1. The first step is take all my furniture, place them in the center of my room and cover them with foil.
2. The second step will be measure whole wall of my room.
3. The third step will be to go to the nearest shop with paints, choose the right shade of color, buy it and of course paint rollers and brushes.
4. The fourth step will be place the paper on the floor in order to avoid unwanted dirt.
5. Now we can start to paint the walls! We must start at the top of the wall and then go lower and lower.
6. After we paint the whole wall, we should check that the paint is clearly visible and it's dry.
7. If it's dry we can take the paper from the floor and remove the foil from furniture.
8. The last step will be placed whole furniture as we want. That's all!
